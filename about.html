We design journeys that connect people, cultures, and purpose

Travel Hub was founded by explorers, researchers, and hospitality experts who wanted to change the way people experience the world. Today we craft travel programs in over 70 countries with sustainability and inclusion at the core.

Our mission

Empowering meaningful travel for every explorer

We curate experiences that support local communities, respect heritage, and help travelers grow. Each itinerary is created alongside destination experts, ensuring authenticity and impact.

  • Hyper-curated itineraries led by local guides and storytellers.
  • 2% of every trip funds community projects via our Impact Fund.
  • Partnerships with eco-certified hotels and responsible operators.
Our story

From a garage travel club to a global collective

2012 · The idea takes flight

Our founders hosted immersive travel salons in San Francisco, connecting explorers with local storytellers via community events.

2015 · First global partnerships

We signed with hospitality brands and airlines to unlock tailored experiences and reliable 24/7 support for our members.

2019 · Impact program launched

Travel Hub Impact Fund started funding clean water, education, and wildlife conservation projects in eight countries.

2024 · 120k+ travelers served

With virtual consultations, hybrid corporate retreats, and carbon-neutral itineraries, we reimagined travel for the post-pandemic era.
